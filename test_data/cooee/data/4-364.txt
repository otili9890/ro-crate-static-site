Bishop of Tasmania.
Bishopscourt, Hobart, October 19th, 1896.
My Dear Sir, - 
I wish your Convention every success, but I fear I have no time or opportunity to give you any aid except good wishes.
Yours faithfully,
H. H. TASMANIA.
Dr. Machattie, M.B.
