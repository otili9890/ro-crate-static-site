I was near to have looked for work when about 3 months ago I caught a chance of finding gold; since I have been making from 3 / 4 to 1 / 2 and sometimes 1 dwt. a day for which I am thankful. I am unable to know how long it will take me to work out my claim, should it continue to pay.
