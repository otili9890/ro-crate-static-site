The Speaker, N.S.W. Parliament.
(TELEGRAM.)
Dr. Machattie. Nov. 19, 1896.
I am very sorry circumstances which were unforeseen up to yesterday prevent me from being present. I am watching with great interest the work of the Convention.
J. P. ABBOTT.
